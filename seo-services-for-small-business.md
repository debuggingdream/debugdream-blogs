## What Are SEO Services and Why Do Small Businesses Need Them?

SEO services encompass a range of strategies designed to improve your website's visibility in search engine results. For small businesses, this means appearing when customers search for your products or services.

### The Core Components of SEO Services

#### On-Page SEO

* Keyword optimization in content
* Meta titles and descriptions
* Header tag structure
* Internal linking architecture
* Image optimization and alt text

#### Technical SEO

* Website speed optimization
* Mobile responsiveness
* SSL security certificates
* XML sitemaps
* Structured data markup

#### Off-Page SEO

* Quality backlink building
* Local citations and directories
* Brand mentions
* Social signals

#### Local SEO (Critical for Nepal-based businesses)

* Google Business Profile optimization
* Local keyword targeting
* NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency
* Local reviews and ratings
* Location-specific content
