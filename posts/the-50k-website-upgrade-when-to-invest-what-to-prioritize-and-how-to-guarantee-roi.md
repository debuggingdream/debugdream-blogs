### Priority 5: The "Why Aren't We Getting Leads?" Fix (Conversion Rate Optimization - CRO)

This is where the magic happens. It’s about continuous improvement based on data.

*   **Problem:** Visitors arrive but don't convert. The path from visitor to lead/sale is broken.
*   **Solution:** Implement robust CRO strategies. This involves:
    *   **A/B Testing:** Experimenting with different headlines, calls to action, button colors, form layouts, and page structures.
    *   **Heatmaps and Session Recordings:** Understanding *how* users interact with your site to identify drop-off points and usability issues.
    *   **Personalization:** Tailoring content and offers based on user behavior or demographics.
    *   **Friction Analysis:** Identifying and removing any element that slows down or complicates the user journey. This is a core part of why your website isn't converting visitors into revenue.
